Artist Terms and Conditions

Artist Agreement

By registering as an artist on Creative Gallery, you agree to the following terms:

  1. Ownership: You warrant that you own all rights to the artwork you upload or have permission to sell it.
  2. Accuracy: You will provide accurate descriptions of your artwork including medium, dimensions, and condition.
  3. Commission: Creative Gallery takes a 15% commission on all sales. You receive 85% of the sale price.
  4. Shipping: For physical artwork, you are responsible for proper packaging and timely shipping to buyers.
  5. Digital Delivery: Digital artwork must be delivered to the buyer within 48 hours of purchase.
  6. Payments: Payments are processed monthly on the 15th for all sales from the previous month.

Content Guidelines

Artwork must not contain:

  • Hate speech, discriminatory content, or harassment of any kind
  • Explicit adult content without proper labeling as "NSFW"
  • Copyrighted material you don't own or have permission to use
  • AI-generated art must be clearly labeled as such in the description
  • Counterfeit items or illegal content

Quality Standards

We maintain high quality standards for our marketplace:

  • Images must be high resolution (minimum 1500px on the longest side)
  • Artwork must be properly photographed with accurate color representation
  • Descriptions should be detailed and include inspiration, medium, and creation process
  • Creative Gallery reserves the right to remove artwork that doesn't meet our quality standards
